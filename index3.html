About Us

EduAI is an intelligent education platform that bridges the gap between students and quality learning. Powered by cutting-edge Large Language Models and AI technology, we provide every student with a personalized tutor that is available 24/7, understands their pace, and adapts to their unique learning style.

Whether you are a student looking for instant doubt resolution, a teacher who wants to save time grading assignments, or an institution aiming to improve outcomes — EduAI is built for you.

About Future
Technology

The future of education is not a classroom — it is a conversation. EduAI uses LangChain Agents and Vector Databases to create an AI tutor that remembers what you have studied, tracks where you are struggling, and builds a learning path tailored specifically for you.

Knowledge Graphs connect concepts across subjects so students can see the bigger picture. Instead of memorising in isolation, learners understand how topics relate, which leads to deeper, long-lasting understanding. This is not the future — this is available today.

Our
Features

EduAI combines multiple AI-powered capabilities into a single unified platform so that students, teachers, and institutions can achieve more with less effort.

01

AI Tutor powered by LLMs that answers student doubts instantly, explains concepts in simple language, and adjusts its depth based on the learner's level.

02

Personalised learning paths that recommend courses, projects, and tutorials based on your goals, past performance, and areas that need improvement.

03

Automatic quiz and assessment generation from any topic or document — saving teachers hours of manual work while keeping questions relevant and varied.

04

AI-generated study notes and assignment evaluation with detailed feedback so students know exactly what to improve and why their answer was marked accordingly.

Our Vision

01

Make quality education accessible to every student regardless of their location, background, or financial situation — by putting an expert AI tutor in their pocket that is always available and never impatient.

02

Empower teachers to focus on what humans do best — inspire, mentor, and connect — while AI handles repetitive tasks like grading, quiz creation, and performance tracking automatically.

Our Mission

01

Deliver an AI assistant that understands student doubts in natural language and provides clear, accurate, step-by-step explanations at any time of day without waiting for a teacher's availability.

02

Analyse student performance continuously using data-driven insights so that weak topics are identified early and extra practice is recommended before they become serious knowledge gaps.

03

Build trust between students, teachers, and technology by providing transparent AI evaluations, honest feedback, and tools that complement human teaching rather than replacing it entirely.
